Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti carbonara is a great dish to add to a beginner chefs repetoire. The steps are simple, and few ingredients
are required in order to have a delicious meal. Note this is a modified version of authentic italian carbonara, so take this with a
grain of salt :)

ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti)
  • Eggs (2-4)
  • Bacon (Sorry to italy)
  • Parmesan (grated)
  • Black Pepper
  • salt

Steps to make

  1. To start break your eggs into a mixing bowl. Add parmesan until fully saturated. Then add a generous amount of pepper
  2. Next, in a separate pan, cook your bacon on low/medium heat from a cold pan to render out the fat
  4. turn your bacon frequently while cooking - I prefer my bacon a little on the tender side for carbonara
  5. Bring a bowl of water to boil, once boiling - add heaps of salt (briney like the ocean!)
  6. Cook your pasta for about ten minutes or until desired rigidity (al dente is my preference)
  7. Move your pasta to your bacon pan and lightly toss. Then return to the original bowl after pouring out the water
  9. It is also recommended to have some starch water left for use - but be careful with the salt content
  10. In your big pan, add your egg mixture and mix like crazy, until desired creaminess is achieved

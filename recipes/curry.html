Japanese Curry

Description

Japanese curry is a delicious dish that is very simple to make. With a few ingredients and about 30 minutes, you can make a nice meal that will feed you for days.
Japanese curry is very hearty and in my opinion, the spicier you opt for the better. Below I will outline the ingredients needed, as well as the steps to make the curry for yourself.

Ingredients

  • Curry blocks (extra spicy is optional but recommended)
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Cauliflower (optional)
  • Ground Beef (I prefer Beyond Meat)
  • Onion
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a pan on medium heat, cook beyond meat till brown
  3. Season with salt and pepper, some cumin won't hurt either
  4. In a dutch oven add some olive oil and heat on medium
  5. Sautee onions till clear, then add other vegetables and sautee for 5 minutes
  6. After sauteeing, add water to cover the vegetables and bring to a low boil
  7. Reduce heat to simmer and cover the pot then allow to cook for 15 minutes
  8. Once the 15 minutes have elapsed remove heat and slowly add curry blocks
  9. Stir often and add blocks until you achieve your desired consistency
  10. Add your ground beef to your curry, stir and enjoy!

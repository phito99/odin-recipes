Japanese Curry

Description

Japanese curry is a delicious dish that is very simple to make. With a few ingredients and about 30 minutes, you can make a dish that will feed you for days. Japanese curry is very hearty and in my opinion, the spicier you opt for the better. Below I will outline the ingredients needed, as well as the steps to make the curry for yourself.

Ingredients

  • Curry blocks (extra spicy is optional but recommended)
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Cauliflower (optional)
  • Ground Beef (I prefer Beyond Meat for this)
  • Water

Brussel Sprouts

Description

Brussel sprouts are a highly underrated vegetable and the most delicious in my opinion. Making them is very easy as well
and they can be made in a variety of ways. I will walk you through one of my tried and true methods for making delicious sprouts.

ingredients

  • Brussel Sprouts (hopefully obvious)
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees farenheit
  2. Cut the base of the brussels off and cut them in half
  3. Lie them face up in a pan lined with foil or parchment paper
  4. Lightly drizzle olive oil over the sprouts, then balsamic vinegar
  5. Season the sprouts with salt, pepper, and some garlic powder
  6. Lightly drizzle some honey over the sprouts as desired
  7. Place your brussels in the oven for 15 minutes
  8. After 15 minutes, remove from oven and shuffle them around. Then cook for 15 more minutes
  9. Once your brussels have achieved a dark brown color from baking remove and enjoy (after cooling down a smidge).
